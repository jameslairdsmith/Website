Visualizations

This page presents a collection of data visualizations I’ve created, mostly for fun/in my free time. All visualizations were created using the R programming language.

Maynard James Keenan Songs

I gathered data through the Spotify API to analyze the length of songs by Maynard James Keenan’s bands.


RVA Pets

Using data from Richmond’s Open Data Portal and Open Street Map, I mapped the location of cats and dogs in RVA.


Your Mom’s House Podcast

I mined transcripts from the YMH Youtube channel to look at definitive words – those that are unique to a group of episodes – over the last ~150 episodes.


Beach Volleyball

Using data from the #TidyTuesday Project (via BigTime stats), I visualized Kerri Walsh Jennings and Misty May-Treanor’s key match statistics over time.


Beer Production

Using data from the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, I created a cartogram showing which states produce the most beer relative to their populations.


White Christmas Lyrics

After pulling lyrics using the {genius} package, I created a network to show connections between words used in the album.


Virginia Early Childhood Care & Education

I used data from the Virginia Quality website and Google’s GeoCoding API to create a Voronoi tesselation of high-quality ECCE sites in Virginia.
